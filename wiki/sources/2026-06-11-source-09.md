# Source: MVP_GUARDRAILS

## Summary
Ingested and normalized MVP gate constraints covering scope boundaries, anti-drift rules, anti-UI/image/camera limits, and blocked capabilities for PR0 and the following MVP sequence.

## Key points
- MVP must remain deterministic core work: no UI, canvas, adapter as truth, camera/image/vision, plugin, CAD, cloud, marketplace, or beauty/intent scoring.
- `PR0` stays documentation-only and cannot be bypassed by claiming helper/demo/temporary exceptions.
- Emphasizes replay-readiness visibility (`Run`, `PackLock`, `OperationContext`) and hidden tolerance rejection.
- Maintains explicit separation between core rule execution and presentation/transport layers.
