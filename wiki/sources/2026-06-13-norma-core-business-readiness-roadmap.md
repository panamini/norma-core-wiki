Ingested the post-PR25 readiness roadmap defining explicit phase gates, PR sequencing, and non-goals before expanding into broader delivery surfaces.

- Execution is fixed to post-PR25 sequence PR27–PR46 with explicit gating (PR27–29, PR30–32, PR33–35, PR36–39, PR40–43, PR44–46).
- Per-PR discipline includes mandatory scope statements, acceptance criteria, and validation commands.

## Implications

- Guardrails explicitly block forbidden surfaces (full SDK, vision/image, CAD/plugin, publish, product drift) until gated phases complete.
